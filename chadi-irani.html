The Story

Both sides of the law.
One mission — yours.

From defending the disadvantaged at Legal Aid to running serious indictable prosecutions at the DPP, Chadi has stood at every position the criminal justice system offers. That perspective is now your defence.

Chadi began his career at one of Sydney's most respected specialist criminal defence firms, cutting his teeth on the matters most lawyers spend a career trying to land — serious assaults, drugs, fraud, sexual offences.

He then spent years at Legal Aid NSW, defending clients without the means to fight their own battles. Theft, fraud, domestic violence, drug offences, drink driving — the practical realities of NSW criminal law, every day, every court.

The decisive turning point was his appointment as Senior Prosecutor at the Office of the Director of Public Prosecutions. There, he ran the Crown's case in major matters — drugs, robbery, sexual assault, weapons. He learnt exactly how prosecutors think, where briefs break, and which arguments judges accept.

Before law, Chadi worked as a Business Marketing Executive for multi-million-dollar companies. The strategic frame he brings to a Court Attendance Notice is not accidental — it is the product of two careers, one mind.

He now leads Powerhouse Law Australia from Parramatta, appearing across every Local, District and Supreme Court in NSW.
