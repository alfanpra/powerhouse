Solicitor & Counsel

The right barrister for the right brief.

The solicitor-counsel partnership is at the heart of how serious criminal defence is run in New South Wales. We do not simply hand a brief to the nearest available barrister — every match is deliberate.

For contested hearings, defended jury trials and complex sentencing, the choice of counsel is one of the most consequential decisions in the matter. We consider the court, the evidence, the temperament of the bench, and the kind of advocacy the brief demands before briefing a barrister.

We instruct Senior Counsel on matters that warrant silk — the most serious indictable charges, appellate work in the Court of Criminal Appeal, and matters where the facts or law are unsettled. For Local and District Court matters that need a courtroom advocate alongside the solicitor, we brief Junior Counsel with directly relevant trial experience.

Our barristers practice from leading NSW chambers including Sir Owen Dixon Chambers, Trust Chambers, Black Chambers, Wardell Chambers, Samuel Griffith Chambers and Lachlan Macquarie Chambers — Sydney's frontline criminal bar.
